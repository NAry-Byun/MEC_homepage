What is M.E.C RMIT Club?

Welcome to the RMIT Microsoft Enthusiasts Club, where we aim to create an active network of RMIT students passionate about the Microsoft environment. We provide opportunities for members to develop and share their Microsoft skills through peer-to-peer engagement activities and offer support in enhancing their professional profiles by engaging with various Microsoft opportunities. Our activities include practical Microsoft lab simulations and workshops, guest speakers sharing their experiences, participation in challenges like the Microsoft Imagine Cup, and social events to encourage bonding. Join us to enhance your Microsoft skills, build your professional profile, and connect with like-minded peers,
with a new club activity opening every month!
